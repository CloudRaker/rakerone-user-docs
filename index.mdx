---
title: "RakerOne user guide"
description: "Set up document-heavy processes once, then let Florent draft while you review and approve."
---

RakerOne is a back-office automation platform where you and the AI assistant **Florent** run document-heavy processes together — intake, invoice processing, contract review, vendor onboarding, claims, and the like. You define a process once and run it whenever you need it. Florent does the heavy lifting — reading documents, pre-filling forms, drafting records and documents — but **you always review and approve before anything becomes real data.**

In short: Florent drafts; you decide.
